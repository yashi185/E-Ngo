Welcome to

A Virtual NGO Management Website

This project aims to computerize the administration of an NGO. The NMS software is designed as there are several aspects to this, including getting the list of students to help in case of limited funds, collecting funds for stray animals, collecting donations in all forms (stationary, clothes, books etcetera) and maintaining a record of all expenditure. There is also a web portal for the registration of new donors and a blog of the NGO. NMS would help increase productivity of the NGO by reducing the time required in the operations of the NGO. It will also increase the accuracy of the work done.
